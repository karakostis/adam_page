ADAM Earthquakes

Within a few minutes following an earthquake, ADAM automatically creates a dashboard with the most important information on the event. After a couple of hours, a ShakeMap dashboard is generated, as a follow up on possible impact on the population. Subscriptions are open to members of the Humanitarian Community (UN agencies, NGOs, Institutions, Ministries etc.).

ADAM Tropical Storms

ADAM Tropical Storms monitors every significant cyclone worldwide, creating dashboards with maps and cyclone-related information. Moreover, the system performs a detailed analysis on population potentially affected, reported by municipalities and risk levels. Subscriptions are open to members of the Humanitarian Community (UN agencies, NGOs, Institutions, Ministries etc.).

ADAM Earthquakes

An advanced notification system for earthquakes:

  • Near Real Time Email Notifications
  • Alerts by Country, Regions or Global
  • Follow up ShakeMaps for impact estimation
  • Detailed Dashboard
  • Information on population potentially affected
  • Weather Forecast
  • WFP Presence in affected area

ADAM Tropical Storms

An advanced notification system for tropical storms:

  • Tropical Storms Email Notifications
  • Notifications by Country, Regions or Global
  • Regular updates when new forecast becomes available
  • WFP Presence in affected area
  • Information on population potentially affected, reported by risk level and municipality
